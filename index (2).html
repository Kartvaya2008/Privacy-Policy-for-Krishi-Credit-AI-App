Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Krishi-Credit AI collects, uses, stores, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application. Please read this document carefully before using our services.

Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to Krishi-Credit AI ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting the personal information of our users — the farmers, agricultural entrepreneurs, and rural communities who rely on our platform for smarter credit decisions.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you access or use the Krishi-Credit AI mobile application. By using our application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy and agree to its terms.

If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application and contact us at kartvayaraikwar@gmail.com to request deletion of your data.

We believe that financial privacy is a right — not a privilege. Farmers deserve to explore credit options without fear of misuse of their personal or financial information.

Section 02

Data We Collect

To provide our AI-powered credit assessment and agricultural financing services, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

Personal Identification

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN)
  • Mobile number and email address
  • Residential and farm address

Agricultural Information

  • Land holding size and type
  • Crop types and cultivation history
  • Livestock or allied activity details
  • Kisan Credit Card details (if applicable)

Financial Information

  • Bank account details
  • Existing loan obligations
  • Annual income estimates
  • Credit history (via authorised bureaus)

Device and Usage Data

  • Device model and OS version
  • App usage patterns and session data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • IP address and geolocation (if permitted)

We collect only what is strictly necessary to provide our services and comply with regulatory requirements under Indian financial and data protection laws.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to serve you better. Specifically, we use your information for the following purposes:

  • Credit Assessment: Our AI models analyse your agricultural and financial profile to provide personalised credit eligibility scores and loan recommendations.
  • Identity Verification: To comply with KYC (Know Your Customer) norms mandated by RBI guidelines for financial service providers.
  • Service Personalisation: To tailor the application experience to your crop type, region, and farming season.
  • Communication: To send relevant alerts about loan status, crop advisories, and policy updates via SMS, email, or in-app notifications.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent unauthorised access, identity fraud, and financial scams.
  • Product Improvement: To analyse usage patterns in aggregate anonymised form to improve AI model accuracy and application features.
  • Legal Compliance: To fulfil obligations under applicable Indian laws including the IT Act 2000, DPDP Act 2023, and RBI directions.

We do not use your data for advertising profiling, sell it to third-party marketers, or use it in ways unrelated to agricultural credit services.

Section 04

AI Disclaimer

Important Notice Regarding AI-Generated Assessments

Credit scores, loan recommendations, and risk assessments provided by Krishi-Credit AI are generated by machine learning models and are provided for informational purposes only. They do not constitute financial advice.

Our AI systems are designed to assist — not replace — human judgment. Please be aware of the following limitations:

  • AI-generated credit assessments are indicative only and do not guarantee loan approval by any lending institution.
  • Models are trained on historical data and may not fully account for local, seasonal, or individual circumstances.
  • Outputs may contain inaccuracies. Consult a qualified financial advisor before making major decisions.
  • We continuously monitor and update our models to reduce bias and improve accuracy.
  • Final credit decisions rest solely with the lending partner or financial institution, not with Krishi-Credit AI.
Section 05

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational safeguards to protect your personal information:

Encryption in Transit

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 / HTTPS protocols.

Encryption at Rest

Sensitive data stored on our servers is protected using AES-256 standard encryption.

Access Controls

Role-based access ensures that only authorised personnel can access personal data on a need-to-know basis.

Security Audits

We conduct periodic security reviews, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing of our infrastructure.

In the event of a data breach that may affect your rights or freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law within 72 hours of becoming aware of the incident.

Section 06

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Lending Partners: With banks, NBFCs, or microfinance institutions to facilitate your loan application — only with your explicit consent at the time of application.
  • Credit Bureaus: With authorised credit bureaus (CIBIL, Equifax, CRIF) as required for credit assessment and as permitted under RBI norms.
  • Government Bodies: With regulatory authorities such as RBI, NABARD, or state agricultural departments if mandated by law or court order.
  • Technology Service Providers: With trusted cloud hosting and analytics providers who are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, data may transfer to the successor entity. You will be notified in advance.

Any third party receiving your data is contractually required to maintain its confidentiality and not use it beyond the scope of service they provide to us.
